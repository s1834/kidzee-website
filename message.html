About Kidzee



Away from The din and noise of city but well connected to Sendhwa, Kidzee has the perfect environment to free the mind from distraction and thus give ambience for education. The Kidzee Campus is located at Vrindawan Dham a generous 8000 square foot area, landscape to blend harmoniously with the surrounding. The premises and facilities enable the school to excel further in the range of opportunities and experience that can be offered to students. The architecture is outstanding and modern.



Message From Principal


Kidzee believes that each child is unique and at Kidzee Sendhwa we nurture and hone the unique potential of each child. Kidzee is the only play school chain to own a university certified pedagogy – Interactive ILLUME. All the facilitators (teachers) in Kidzee Sendhwa undergo a training by Kidzee to ensure their in-depth understanding of illume concepts so that they can have better interaction with the children in their care. This training empowers the facilitators to be experts in ECCE(Early Childhood Care & Education). For children Kidzee Sendhwa is a home away from home. It is like a grooming school for our little stars. Here, we help them imbibe essential life skills in a safe and friendly environment to make them emotionally stable and sociably
acceptable individuals. We follow multiple pathways to provide each child a fun filled learning experience. In our curriculum we integrate the use of scientifically designed toys and equipments for mental stimulation along with interactive class room teaching. We have numerous extracurricular activities like – skating, dancing, sports, yoga, Taekwondo etc.to develop their gross motor skills. Parents are regularly invited to school so that our children can showcase their achievements as we believe that only a parent-facilitator team can take our children to greater heights as – The children of today are the leaders of tomorrow.
