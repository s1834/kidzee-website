Legacy





In our decade-long legacy of nurturing the ‘unique potential’ of every child, we have shaped, in metros and suburbs alike, a new cult of schooling for toddlers. On this revolutionary journey, we evidenced and impacted the much-needed shift from ‘teachers’ quieting an assembly line of children to ‘enablers’ shaping children into smart-thinking, dynamic, and ‘ready-for-life’ individuals. We value our commitment towards quality child education along with complementing aspects of self-reliance, peer interaction, and individual growth. In this process, we created a solid foundation in curriculum and polished our business model to make it profitable for all the stakeholders involved.
